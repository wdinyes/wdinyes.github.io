Over the weekend I was listening to the radio while driving somewhere of zero practical importance to the narrative, and heard an extremely frustrating PSA from [Every Mother Counts](https://everymothercounts.org/).  The key fact was this, taken from their website:

> The U.S. is the only high-income country with a consistently rising maternal mortality rate, despite spending more per capita on health care than any other country in the world. Racial and ethnic inequities remain rampant, with Black and Indigenous women being 2-3x more likely than white women to die from complications of pregnancy and birth.

I mean, is it really that hard to see why this is?

A for-profit medical insurance industry that ensures we pay the most in the world for medical coverage and get the least effective results.  Could be solved with what every other "high-income" country has done: socialized medicine.  But, well, you know, *Republicans*.

A Christo-fascist stream of Bible-thumping nonsense about making abortion illegal.  Abortions are medical care.  Your "god" has nothing to do with it.  Don't want one?  Don't have one.  But, well, you know, *Republicans*.

Entrenched racism (that has most recently resulted in the gutting of the Voting Rights Act) feeds into the multiplier for non-white women dying.  But, well, you know, *Republicans*.

Let's not even get into the recent spate of babies dying thanks to misinformation and science-denying about vitamin K shots.  Literally lifesaving. But, well, you know, *Republicans*.

Let me know if you figure out the pattern here.

I'm sure they'll tell you it's not a war on women.
